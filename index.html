About

Research at the intersection of systems neuroscience and tool building

I am a neuroscientist and engineer interested in neural circuit dynamics, behavior, sleep, and neurodegeneration. My work combines experimental neuroscience with quantitative analysis, hardware development, and computational modeling.

I am especially interested in how population activity and internal state shape behavior, and in building tools that make those dynamics measurable in freely moving systems.
