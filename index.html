Site Overview

A research website for extracting meaning from neural dynamics

This site is organized around a central question in systems neuroscience: what do neural dynamics actually mean for computation, internal state, and behavior? It brings together research directions, publications, tutorials, software, and laboratory tools to make that question more concrete, measurable, and experimentally useful.
